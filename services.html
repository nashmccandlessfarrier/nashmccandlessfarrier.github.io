At McCandless Farrier Service we are able to accomplish a wide range of services for your horse from the basic trim to specially designed shoes to help an injured or overdue horse get back into shape.

Trimming

A very important part of hoof care is the trim to keep a well balanced hoof. This is something that requires an appropriate schedule to maintain that balance over time.

Shoeing

If shoes are needed for your horse you can bet we will fit it perfectly. Without proper fitting many problems can arise which is could lead to danger for your horses well being. This is why our shoes are heated to be shaped properly to fit your horses foot everytime at McCandless Farrier Service.

Shows

We are also available to be on-site farriers at shows or small events which might require adjustments, fixing, or re-fitting of shoes.
